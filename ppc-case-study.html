Case Study: Paid Media

High-Impact PPC Optimization for E-commerce

A deep dive into how strict budget management, audience segmentation, and A/B testing achieved significant Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).

The Challenge

Wasting budget on generic keywords with high competition, leading to low Quality Scores.

Lack of clear segmentation between prospecting and remarketing audiences, leading to inefficient bidding.

Mobile campaigns performed poorly due to slow landing page load times and intrusive ad copy.

My Solution

1. Long-Tail Keyword Segmentation: Shifted 60% of the budget to highly specific, low-volume keywords with high buyer intent, boosting Quality Scores.

2. Geo-Fencing & Time Bidding: Implemented precise geo-fencing and optimized bid multipliers for peak conversion times, reducing wasted spend by 15%.

3. Creative Iteration and Mobile Focus: Ran 8 weeks of continuous A/B testing on ad copy, prioritizing simplified, faster landing pages for mobile users.
