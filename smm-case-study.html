Case Study

Data-led Social Media Growth Plan

An exploration of how data-driven insights, timely engagement, and trend-based content fueled rapid social media growth.

The Challenge

Heavy reliance on paid ads for bottom-of-funnel conversions with minimal organic reach.

Niche products relied on generic static posts, lacking engagement or differentiation.

Low conversion rates from link clicks.

My Solution

1. Content Strategy Revamp: Shifted to an 80/20 model — 80% audience-interest content, 20% brand promotions — to boost engagement and trust.

2. Community Integration: Identified and engaged with niche online communities, using social listening and trend hijacking to drive organic traffic.

3. Creative Optimisation: Redesigned video production workflows and trained the team to create high-quality short-form videos faster, improving consistency and performance.
